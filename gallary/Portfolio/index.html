Why I love to code

When I was young my favorite movie was The Matrix. The special effects and action filled plot was enough to keep my curious mind entertained. As I've grown older, the movie has still been one of my all time favorites. In my opinion the message is still sound. We as programmers must take responsibility for what we produce, and ensure that we don't create something that may seem helpful now, but could be bad for society in the future.
